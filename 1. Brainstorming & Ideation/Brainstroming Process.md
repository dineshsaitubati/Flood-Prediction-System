## Brainstorming and Project Selection

During the brainstorming phase, multiple ideas related to natural disaster prediction were explored. Various domains, including earthquake prediction, cyclone forecasting, drought analysis, and flood prediction, were evaluated based on data availability, feasibility, and potential impact.

After careful evaluation, **Flood Prediction** was selected for the following reasons:

- Historical datasets were publicly available.
- Floods have a significant social impact.
- Machine Learning performs well on structured weather datasets.
- The project combines Data Science and Web Development to build a complete end-to-end application.
