Floods are one of the most frequent and destructive natural disasters, causing severe damage to human life, infrastructure, agriculture, and the economy. Every year, many regions experience heavy rainfall and overflowing rivers, resulting in large-scale destruction. Although weather forecasting has improved over the years, accurately identifying flood-prone situations before they occur remains a major challenge.
Traditional flood prediction methods often rely on manual observations or rule-based systems that may not effectively analyze multiple weather parameters simultaneously. With the advancement of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, predictive systems can analyze historical weather data to identify patterns associated with flood events.
The idea behind this project emerged from the need to develop a simple, intelligent, and accessible flood prediction system that can assist authorities and disaster management teams in making faster and more informed decisions.
